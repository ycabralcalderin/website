About

Cognitive Neuroscientist at Freie Universität Berlin working on perception, consciousness, and neural oscillations across sensory and cognitive domains.

My research combines psychophysics, EEG/MEG, intracranial recordings, fMRI, and non-invasive brain stimulation to understand how brain rhythms shape conscious experience.
